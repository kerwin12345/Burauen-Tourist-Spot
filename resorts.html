RESORTS

CAMP CAWAYAN RESORT

You won’t only enjoy the swimming pools, Camp Kawayan also have such relaxing view and fresh air considering that this resort is located at the province and there is a lot of trees surrounding the place.

VILLA MONTALLA RESORT

A resort located at Burauen, Leyte where you can relax and enjoy sqimming at our 700sqm pool. Huge pool, that you can stretch your tired muscle.

YANDA POOL RESORT

Yanda Pool Resorts, nestled in the heart of Burauen, Leyte, is a tropical paradise that offers a refreshing getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life.This resort is renowned for its crystal-clear swimming pools, set amidst lush greenery and picturesque landscapes.

CANSIBOY BURABOD MOUNTAIN RESORT

The resort has a man-made pool with spring waters coming exclusively from mountain peaks. The amount of spring water it pours down to the hillside probably depends on the water pressure in the aquifer or the amount of rainwater.

EL MONTANO VISTA RESORT

El Montaño Vista Resort, located in the scenic town of Burauen, Leyte, is a haven for those seeking a serene and rejuvenating retreat. This resort is celebrated for its stunning vistas, tranquil ambiance, and top-notch amenities

MAHAGNAO LAKE VIEW RESORT

Mahagnao Lake View Resort, situated in the charming town of Burauen, Leyte, is a hidden gem that offers a tranquil escape from the everyday hustle. This resort is famous for its breathtaking views of the serene Mahagnao Lake, surrounded by lush greenery and a peaceful atmosphere. It's the perfect place for nature lovers, adventure seekers, or anyone looking for a relaxing getaway.
