BIG DADDY'S CUP

Big Daddy's Cup, located in the heart of Burauen, Leyte, is a cherished local gem that serves up a delightful array of beverages. Known for its warm atmosphere and friendly staff, this spot is perfect for a morning coffee, an afternoon tea, or a refreshing smoothie.

ANDOKS BURAUEN

Andok's in Burauen, Leyte, is a popular dining spot known for its delicious and affordable Filipino cuisine. This restaurant is famous for its mouth-watering Lechon Manok and Dokito Frito, which have become favorites among locals and tourists alike.

CRISPY KING

Crispy King, located in Burauen, Leyte, is a beloved restaurant that specializes in serving crispy and flavorful dishes. With a menu that includes a variety of mouth-watering options, Crispy King is a go-to spot for locals and visitors alike. From their signature crispy fried chicken to other delectable offerings, every bite is sure to satisfy your taste buds.
